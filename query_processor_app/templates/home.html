Welcome to StreetWise. Please note that this site is under development and information is not guaranteed to be accurate!

Durham Streetwise is an information lookup tool being created for Durham residents. The site will the end user to enter their Durham home address and receive a wide variety of useful information such as nearest library, nearest hospital and polling location. We are creating this site to benefit those who are not internet savvy or do not have readily available internet access.

StreetWise is geared toward Durham residents who are not web savvy, or do not have readily available internet access. The site will be simple, responsive and will generate a printable PDF that can be kept for future reference. The site could also benefit most anyone who has recently moved to a new Durham address.
