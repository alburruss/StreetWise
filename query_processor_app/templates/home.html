Welcome to the StreetWise demo. Please note, this site is under development and information is not guaranteed to be accurate!

Streetwise is an information lookup tool being created for Durham residents. The site will allow the end user to enter their Durham address and receive a wide variety of useful information. StreetWise is being made to benefit those who are not internet savvy or do not have readily available internet access.
